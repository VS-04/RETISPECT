Instructions:

  • Ensure proper lighting before starting the test.
  • Maintain a distance of 3-5 feet from the screen.
  • Close one eye at a time while reading the letters.
  • Start from the top row and move downward.
  • If you struggle with smaller rows, consider an eye check-up.

Test Description:

  • This test evaluates your ability to identify letters at different sizes.
  • Larger letters are easier to read, while smaller ones indicate vision sharpness.
  • It helps detect possible vision defects like myopia or hyperopia.
  • This is a basic vision test and does not replace a professional eye exam.
